The Colombian – Pre-Lit Artificial Christmas Tree – Snow Flocked Green – Warm White Lights

“The Colombian”is a snow flocked and hinged artificial Christmas tree which has 770 warm white LED lights already attached. The LED lights have one still mode, with no flashing. It has a mixture of dark green PVC and PE branches which provide a striking contrast to the bright white snow flocking.  Being hinged and pre-lit, this Christmas tree is perfect for customers who are looking for a simply stunning Christmas tree with very little assembly. Please note: Christmas tree in this photo has been assembled and is sitting in our rotating stand (cost of stand additional).
