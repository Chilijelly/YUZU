The Colombian – Pre-Lit Artificial Christmas Tree – Snow Flocked Green – Warm White Lights – 6 foot

“The Colombian” Pre-Lit 6ft (1.8m) is a snow flocked and hinged artificial Christmas tree which has 600 warm white LED lights already attached. The LED lights have one still mode, with no flashing. It has a mixture of dark green PVC and PE branches which provide a striking contrast to the bright white snow flocking.  Being hinged and pre-lit, this Christmas tree is perfect for customers who are looking for a simply stunning Christmas tree with very little assembly.

  • Height – 6ft (1.8 metres)
  • Width – Approximately 118cm
  • Number of Tips – 2547
  • Number of Styles of Tips – 3
  • Style of Tree – Hinged
  • Colour – Foliage – Dark Green
  • Colour – Lights – Warm White
  • Number of Lights – 600
  • Branches fall close to the ground
  • Heavy duty metal stand
