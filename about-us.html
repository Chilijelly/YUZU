About Us

About Us
We are a China-based company specializing in the design, manufacturing, and sales of Christmas trees. Committed to delivering high-quality products to the global market, we have earned widespread recognition and trust through our exceptional designs, superb craftsmanship, and excellent service.

Our Partners
Over the years, we have established long-term partnerships with numerous world-renowned retailers and international governments, including prominent companies such as Walmart and Target. Our products are distributed worldwide, bringing joy and warmth to countless households during the festive season.

Our Advantages
30 Years of Experience: With our extensive industry experience, we deeply understand market demands and continuously introduce innovative products that align with market trends.
Complete Supply Chain: From design and manufacturing to sales, we own a complete supply chain, ensuring that every aspect of product quality meets the highest standards.
Excellent Customer Service: We prioritize customer needs, offering personalized services to ensure every customer enjoys the most satisfying shopping experience.

Covering 30.000 square meters, Zhejiang Yuzu Christmas Tree Company locates at TaiZhou city, Zhejiang province, China. It takes 30 minutes from Ningbo by train, and 40 minutes to Shanghai by air. Every day there is scheduled flight to Huangyan from Shanghai and Guangzhou.

Established in 1994, Zhejiang Yuzu Christmas Tree Company mainly manufactures artificial Christmas trees, garlands, wreathes and decoration trees. The company has targeted good quality and competitive price for 30 years.

Our annual sales have reached $5,000,000 facilitated by ten production lines and backed up by 200 workers and staff members. We are one of the largest producers of Christmas products in China. Our products have been exported to many foreign countries such as the U.S., Spain, France and Greece.
