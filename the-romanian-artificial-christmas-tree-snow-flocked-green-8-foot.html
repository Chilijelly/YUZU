The Romanian – Artificial Christmas Tree – Snow Flocked Green – 8 foot

“The Romanian” 8ft (2.4 m) is a snow flocked and hinged artificial Christmas tree.  It has a mixture of PVC and PE branches which combine to make a stunning and very realistic impression of a tree covered in snow during Winter in the Alps.  Our customers who have visited our showroom display have nominated this Christmas tree as the top of our range.

  • Height – 8ft (2.4 metres)
  • Width – Approximately 155 cm
  • Number of Tips – 5508
  • Number of Styles of Tips – 3
  • Style of Tree – Hinged
  • Colour – Green with snow flock
  • Branches fall close to the ground
  • Heavy duty metal stand
