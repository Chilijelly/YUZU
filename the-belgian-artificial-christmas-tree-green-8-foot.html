The Belgian – Artificial Christmas Tree – Green – 8 foot

“The Belgian” 8ft (2.4 m) is a hinged artificial Christmas tree with a mixture of dark green PVC and PE branches which combine to give a very textured realistic appearance and has proven to be popular with customers who do not want a lot of assembly with all the branches already connected to the centre pole with metal hinges.  This Christmas tree also has a slightly slimmer appearance with a smaller diameter for customers concerned about floor space.

  • Height – 8ft (2.4 metres)
  • Width – Approximately 142 cm
  • Number of Tips – 2222
  • Number of Styles of Tips – 3
  • Style of Tree – Hinged
  • Colour – Dark Green
  • Branches fall close to the ground
  • Heavy duty metal stand
