The Canadian – Cone Style Christmas Tree – Slim Frame – Green – 20 foot (6m)

“The Canadian” Cone Style Artificial Christmas Tree is a modular design with a steel cone frame and built-in ladder.  It has long branches with a mixture of PVC traditional and needle pine branches of three slightly varied shades of dark green which combine to make what is by far our most popular traditional style and most realistic dark green Christmas tree labelled by our customers as a “timeless classic”.  This model has a slim frame and is a magnificent structure for any building.  This design is also suitable for outdoors and an engineer’s certificate is available if required. Please note tree in image is decorated and are not included in the price.
