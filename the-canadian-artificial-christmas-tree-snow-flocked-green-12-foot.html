The Canadian – Artificial Christmas Tree – Snow Flocked Green – 12 foot

“The Canadian” 12ft (3.6 m) is a hooked style Christmas tree.  It has a mixture of PVC traditional and needle pine branches of three slightly varied shades of dark green which combine to make what is by far our most popular traditional style and most realistic dark green Christmas tree labelled by our customers as a “timeless classic” – suitable for use in both domestic and commercial environments. This provides a striking contrast to the bright white snow flocking.  This style Christmas tree was one of our very first two designs and has also been selected by installers across the country and used in countless commercial displays as well as consistently selected by customers who have visited our showroom.

  • Height – 12ft (3.6 metres)
  • Width – Approximately 210 cm
  • Number of Tips – 5348
  • Number of Styles of Tips – 3
  • Style of Tree – Hooked (Hinged and Pre-Lit Models also available in selected sizes)
  • Colour – Dark Green and White
  • Branches fall close to the ground
  • Heavy duty metal stand
