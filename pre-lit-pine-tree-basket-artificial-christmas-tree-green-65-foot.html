Pre-lit Pine Tree Basket – Artificial Christmas Tree – Green – 6.5 foot

“The Pre-lit Pine Tree with Basket” 6.5ft (200 cm) is a hinged style artificial Christmas tree. The Pine tree is made from a mixture of PVC and PE branches with two different tips, ranging between a dark green to a light green on the tips. The warm white LED lights are already assembled and combined with a weaved basket to bring a touch of warmth and elegance to your display. Being hinged, this tree is perfect for customers looking for a simple Christmas Tree with a quick and easy assembly.

  • Height – Approximately 6.5ft (200 cm)
  • Width – Approximately 190 cm
  • Number of Tips – 2094
  • Number of Styles of Tips – 2
  • Colour – Green
  • Lights – 300 Warm White LED Lights
  • Heavy duty green metal stand
  • Weaved basket around stand
  • Assembly instruction sheet included as well as an assembly video available here
