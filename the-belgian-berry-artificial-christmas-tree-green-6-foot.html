The Belgian Berry – Artificial Christmas Tree – Green – 6 foot

“The Belgian Berry” 6ft (1.8m) is a hinged artificial Christmas tree.  In this model, our Belgian’s mixture of PVC and PE branches have been pre-decorated with traditional red berries and pine cones which combined with the fact that this is a hinged Christmas tree, result in an easy “all-in-one” solution requiring no further decorations (unless you would like to light it up).  Also, it is easy to add a few extra ornaments for customers wanting to change the look year after year.

  • Height – 6ft (1.8 metres)
  • Width – Approximately 104 cm
  • Number of Tips – 1062
  • Number of Styles of Tips – 3
  • Style of Tree – Hinged
  • Colour – Dark Green
  • Branches fall close to the ground
  • Heavy duty metal stand
  • The Christmas tree is shipped in a strong cardboard box which can be used for storage of the Christmas tree the following year
  • Matching Accessories available – wreaths and garlands
