The Canadian – Christmas Wreath – Green – 90 cm

Matching The Canadian Christmas Tree and Garlands, we have complementing Christmas Wreaths with a mixture of PVC traditional and needle pine branches of three slightly varied shades of dark green which combine to make what is by far our most popular traditional style and realistic “timeless classic” – suitable for use in both domestic and commercial environments. The Canadian style was one of our very first two designs and has also been selected by installers across the country and used in countless commercial displays as well as consistently selected by customers who have visited our showroom.  It can be hung on a door  or laid flat on a table as a traditional foliage centrepiece.
