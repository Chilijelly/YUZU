The Norwegian – Artificial Christmas Tree – Green

“The Norwegian” is a hinged style Christmas tree.  It has a mixture of PVC traditional and needle pine branches of three varied shades of green – from light to dark green, which combine to make a very realistic Christmas tree – suitable for use in both domestic and commercial environments. Being hinged, this Christmas tree is great for customers who enjoy a quick and easy assembly.
