The Austrian – Artificial Christmas Tree – Green – 8 foot

The Needle Pine Christmas Tree stands at 8ft (2.4 metres) tall. The PVC pine style branches provide a soft tone and feel to this traditional artificial Christmas tree.   • Height – 8ft (2.4 metres)
  • Width – Approximately 152 cm
  • Number of Tips – 1489
  • Style of Tree – Hinged
  • Colour – Foliage – Green
  • Branches fall above the ground with space for presents underneath
  • Heavy duty green metal stand
  • The Christmas tree is shipped in a strong cardboard box which can be used for storage of the Christmas tree the following year
